Island Jazz
Art Direction
Branding Design
Photography
Motion Design
Azymuth is a legendary brazilian jazz/funk trio formed in 1968. A dream came true when we had the chance to realise an event hosting them on an island near Berlin. Is- land Jazz was born.
The goal was to emphasize on the fun aspects about jazz and/or jazz funk. Thus, we decided to go for a loud, colorful and playful visual language for the event was needed. In addition I directed, shot and edited a promotional teaser to underline the natural aspect of the venue. This was distributed digitally but also with posters around the city at relevant spaces.
